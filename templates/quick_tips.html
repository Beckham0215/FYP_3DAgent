💡 Quick Tips & User Guide

Learn how to make the most of 3DAgent's features for exploring and organizing your spaces.

← Back to Dashboard

🚀 Getting Started

📍

1. Create Your First Space

From the dashboard, click "➕ New Space" and enter your Matterport Space ID (SID). You can find this in the space's URL or from the space creator.

Format: Usually a long alphanumeric string
👁️

2. Open the 3D Viewer

Click "Open Viewer" on any space card. The viewer loads with your 3D environment and AI assistant ready to help.

💻 Tip: Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile
🎯

3. Explore the Layout

Familiarize yourself with the viewer layout:

  • 📍 Location Tracker (top-left) - Shows current position
  • 🏷️ Quick Tag Panel (bottom-left) - Mark locations instantly
  • 💬 AI Chat (right) - Talk to your assistant
🔄

4. Minimize Panels as Needed

Click the "−" button on any panel to minimize it. Your preferences are saved automatically. Click "+" to expand again.

✓ State persists even after refresh

🏷️ Marking Locations (Most Important!)

💡 Pro Tip: Mark key locations first (Kitchen, Bedroom, Office, Living Room). This unlocks activity-based navigation and makes future exploration much easier.

⌨️

Method 1: Quick Tag Panel

Fastest way to mark:

  1. Navigate to a location
  2. Look at bottom-left panel
  3. Enter location name (e.g., "Kitchen")
  4. Optionally add category (e.g., "Room")
  5. Click "✓ Save Location"
🎤

Method 2: Voice Command

Use natural language:

  • "Mark this as Kitchen"
  • "Tag this place as Bedroom"
  • "Label this as Office"
  • "Help me mark this location as Living Room"
✓ The AI will confirm when done
⚙️

Method 3: Asset Management Page

For bulk editing:

  1. Click "Manage Assets" from dashboard
  2. Section 1: Navigation Locations
  3. Enter: Location name, Sweep UUID, category
  4. Click "✓ Add Location"
✏️

Edit or Delete Locations

Need to update a location?

  1. Go to "Manage Assets"
  2. Find the location in the table
  3. Click "✏️ Edit" to modify
  4. Click "🗑️" to delete

🗺️ AI-Powered Navigation

🎯

Navigate to Marked Locations

Once you've marked locations, use these commands:

  • "Take me to the kitchen"
  • "Go to bedroom 1"
  • "Navigate to the office"
  • "Show me the living room"
⚠️ Location must be marked first
🍳

Activity-Based Navigation (SMART!)

Say what you want to do, not where to go:

  • "I'm hungry" → 🍽️ Kitchen
  • "I want to cook" → 🍳 Kitchen
  • "I want to sleep" → 🛏️ Bedroom
  • "I need to work" → 💼 Office
  • "I want to relax" → 🛋️ Living Room
  • "I need a shower" → 🚿 Bathroom
✨ Most natural way to navigate
💬

Natural Conversation

The AI understands context:

  • "What's in this room?"
  • "Tell me about the layout"
  • "Where can I find the bathroom?"
  • "How many bedrooms?"
📝 Ask questions freely
🔄

Chat Tips

Getting the best results:

  • Use clear, natural language
  • Be specific about location/action
  • The AI learns from your patterns
  • Rephrase if first attempt doesn't work

📦 Asset & Inventory Management

📍

Two Types of Assets

Navigation Locations: Marked sweeps for voice navigation

Scanned Assets: Inventory items discovered by the AI

🔍

View Your Assets

  1. Click "Manage Assets" from any space
  2. Section 1: All your marked locations
  3. Section 2: Scanned inventory by location
  4. Edit or delete as needed
🔗

Using Sweep UUIDs

Sweep UUID is the unique ID for each location:

  • Shown in Location Tracker (top-left of viewer)
  • Used for precise navigation
  • Copy from tracker and paste when adding manually
🏷️

Categories & Organization

Use categories to organize:

  • Room, Bathroom, Kitchen, Bedroom
  • Living Space, Office, Outdoor
  • Create your own custom categories
  • Helps with large spaces

📊 Typical Asset Management Workflow

  1. Mark Locations: Say "Mark this as Kitchen" in 5-6 key rooms
  2. Navigate: Use activity-based commands like "I'm hungry"
  3. Review: Check "Manage Assets" to see all marked locations
  4. Organize: Add descriptions and categories for clarity
  5. Maintain: Update or delete outdated marks as needed

⭐ Pro Tips & Best Practices

✨

Tip 1: Mark Locations Systematically

Go room-by-room and mark each location. Start with 3-4 main areas (Kitchen, Bedroom, Office, Living Room) for best results.

🎯

Tip 2: Use Activity Commands First

Instead of "Take me to kitchen", say "I'm hungry". Activity-based navigation is more intuitive and helps the AI understand context.

📝

Tip 3: Add Descriptions for Clarity

For spaces with multiple similar rooms, add descriptions like "Master Bedroom with Ensuite" vs just "Bedroom". Helps you and the AI understand better.

🔄

Tip 4: Review Regularly

Check your "Manage Assets" page weekly. Update outdated marks and remove duplicates to keep everything organized.

💬

Tip 5: Use Natural Language

The AI understands conversational commands. Say "I'm tired" instead of "Go to bedroom", or "What's here?" instead of "List items".

🏷️

Tip 6: Consistency is Key

Always use the same names for locations. "Kitchen" not "kitch" or "cook area". Consistency helps the AI recognize and navigate accurately.

🌍

Tip 7: Organize by Categories

Use categories to group similar locations. Mark multiple bathrooms with category "Bathroom" for easy organization.

⚡

Tip 8: Minimize Unused Panels

While exploring, minimize panels you don't need. This gives more screen space to the 3D viewer and makes navigation smoother.

🔧 Troubleshooting & FAQ

❓ AI won't navigate to a location I marked

Solution:

  • Check that the location has been marked (go to "Manage Assets")
  • Use the exact location name when asking ("Kitchen" not "kitch")
  • Try an activity command instead: "I'm hungry" instead of "Go to kitchen"
  • Refresh the page and try again
❓ Location Tracker shows no Sweep UUID

Solution:

  • Make sure you're inside the 3D viewer
  • Give the page a moment to load
  • Refresh the page
  • Try a different browser
  • Check browser console (F12) for errors
❓ 3D space won't load

Solution:

  • Check your internet connection
  • Verify the Matterport Space ID is correct
  • Try a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
  • Clear browser cache: Ctrl+Shift+Del
  • Ensure WebGL is enabled in your browser
❓ Chat panel not responding

Solution:

  • Check internet connection
  • Refresh the page
  • Try a shorter, simpler message first
  • Wait a moment for server response
  • Sign out and back in
❓ Panels are overlapping on my screen

Solution:

  • Use minimize buttons to hide panels you're not using
  • On mobile, try rotating your device
  • Panels automatically adjust on smaller screens
  • Try browser zoom: Ctrl+0 to reset
❓ Can't mark a location

Solution:

  • Make sure you enter a location name in the form
  • Try using the Asset Management page instead
  • Ensure the space is fully loaded
  • Refresh and try again
❓ How many locations can I mark?

Answer: Unlimited! Mark as many locations as you need. There's no practical limit to how many places you can tag.

❓ Do you work on mobile?

Answer: Yes! 3DAgent works on tablets and phones. The layout adapts automatically:

  • ✅ iPhone, iPad (iOS)
  • ✅ Android phones and tablets
  • ✅ Landscape and portrait modes
❓ Can I download the 3D space?

Answer: No, spaces are streamed directly from Matterport. You can't download them, but you can explore and organize them anytime you're logged in.

❓ Can I share spaces with other users?

Answer: Currently, spaces are private to your account. Sharing features may be added in future updates!
