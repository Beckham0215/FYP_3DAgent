💡 Quick Tips & Guide

Everything 3DAgent can do — the 3D viewer tools, the AI chat, and the maintenance system.

How 3DAgent is organised

Each Space is a Matterport 3D scan. Inside a space you work with two separate kinds of data:

  • 📍 Locations — named navigation points (tagged sweeps) like "Office 1" the AI can take you to.
  • 📦 Assets — individual objects the scanner detects, like "Chair #2", each pinned to where it was seen.

Open a space from the Dashboard, then use the Viewer, the Locations/Assets pages, the Export center, or the Maintenance board.

🧰 The Viewer tools menu (top-left)

🗺

Floor Plan

Opens a per-floor minimap of every sweep, with your current position. Multi-floor spaces are shown one floor at a time so they don't overlap. You can also export an annotated plan.

📷

Scan Area

Runs a 360° scan of the current sweep (or a whole tagged area) to detect objects with AI vision. A loader shows progress; results open in a draggable panel for review and saving.

📍

Location

Tag the current spot, and manage all locations — list, search, navigate (▶), rename, and delete — all in one panel.

🤖

Auto-Tag Locations

Pick sweeps on the floor plan (or "tag every sweep") and the agent walks them, names each area with AI, and tags every sweep — adjacent sweeps in one room share a name (Office 1 #1, Office 1 #2). A results plan lets you hover, rename, and delete.

📦

Assets

Browse scanned inventory by room. Click 🔆 to fly to and precisely outline a specific instance (e.g. Chair #2).

⬇

Export

Download the floor plan, the locations, or the assets as PNG / CSV / PDF.

⚠️

Report Issue

An interactive 3-step flow: pick a location → pick the faulty asset (it flies to & outlines it) → describe it and set severity.

🔧

Problem Equipment

Lists everything reported as faulty in this space. Click an item to fly to it and outline it.

💬 Talk to the AI — anything you can click, you can say

Type (or 🎤 speak) to the assistant in the viewer. It understands commands for every feature:

Navigate & locate

  • "take me to the kitchen"
  • "go to the nearest fire extinguisher"
  • "where am I?"
  • "show the floor plan"
    •

    Ask about assets

  • "how many chairs in this location"
  • "how many tables in the lab"
  • "how many chairs in total"
  • "what assets are here"
  • "what locations are tagged"
    •

    Do things

  • "mark this as Office 2"
  • "scan this area"
  • "auto-tag all locations"
  • "report chair #1 has a broken leg"
  • "what equipment has problems"

    🛠️ Maintenance system

    ⚠️

    Report a fault

    From the viewer's Report Issue tool or by chatting "report chair #1 is broken" — the report is pinned to that asset's exact spot.

    📋

    Triage board

    The Maintenance page (sidebar) lists reports prioritised by severity, with a live badge of open issues. Admins assign a mechanic and move each through Open → Assigned → In Progress → Resolved.

    👤

    Roles

    Workers report issues and see their own; Admins see and manage everything. Choose your role when registering.

    📂 Locations, Assets & Export

    📍

    Locations page

    Full CRUD over navigation locations for a space, with CSV/PDF export. Open it from the sidebar or a space's Locations button.

    📦

    Assets page

    The scanned inventory per room, filterable, with edit/delete and per-instance naming (Chair #1, Chair #2).

    ⬇️

    Export center

    The Export page lets you download locations or assets of any space as CSV or PDF in one place.

    ⭐ Pro tips

    🤖

    Let Auto-Tag do the work

    Run Auto-Tag Locations once on a new space, then fine-tune names in the results plan or the Locations page.

    🔆

    Outlines are per-instance

    Highlighting traces the actual object edges, and Chair #1 vs Chair #2 resolve to different chairs.

    🪟

    Panels are draggable

    The Tools menu and chat can be dragged and minimised; opening Assets/Locations tucks the chat away so nothing overlaps.
